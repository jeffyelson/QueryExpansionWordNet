Liushi Shan (Chinese: 昆仑女神峰; pinyin: Kūnlún nǚshén fēng; lit. 'Kunlun Goddess Peak'), also known as Kunlun Goddess, is a mountain in the Kunlun Mountain Range in China. The mountain is located on the border of the Tibet and Xinjiang provinces of China.
Liushi Shan has an elevation of 7,167 metres (23,514 ft) and is the highest mountain of the Kunlun Range.
